FireCull is designed to speed up your first pass — always review final selects.

FireCull applies star ratings and colour labels based on photo sharpness and eye states to help you quickly identify your best shots.

These builds run locally inside Lightroom Classic. No images are uploaded or sent anywhere.

Thanks for being part of FireCull Early Access — your feedback helps shape the product.

How FireCull rates & labels photos

  • Star ratings stay relative within each run so sharper frames float to the top.
    ★★★★★ → sharpest ~20%
    ★★★★ → next ~20%
    ★★★ → middle ~20%
    ★★ → softer ~20%
    ★ → least sharp ~20%
  • Color labels stay simple:
    • Red label = eyes detected as closed.
    • Yellow label = eyes detected as occluded (e.g. sunglasses).
    • No label = eyes open or no face detected.
